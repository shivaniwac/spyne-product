Sales — Condensed Version (Required Questions Only)

⏱ 15–20 min

Pull dashboard data beforehand so the conversation focuses on confirmation and qualitative depth rather than number recall.

Setup & Context 2 min

  • Setup Which Vini products are live, how long, how many locations, franchise or independent?
  • Q1 How many vehicles sold or serviced per month?

The Problem 4 min

  • Q3 Were after-hours sales calls being handled, and what happened to them?
  • Q5 What percentage of sales calls were being missed or poorly handled?
  • Q6 How quickly were inbound calls and internet leads responded to?
  • Q7 Did delayed responses affect conversion?

Before Benchmarks 2 min

  • Q8 What percentage of inbound sales calls turned into qualified leads?
  • Q9 Of qualified leads, how many converted into appointments?

Vini Performance 4 min

  • Q10 How many calls is Vini handling per month?
  • Q11 Qualification rate from Vini-handled calls?
  • Q12 Appointment conversion rate from qualified leads?
  • Q13 After-hours vs. peak overflow split?

Business Impact 3 min

  • Q14 What percentage of Vini appointments would have been missed entirely?
  • Q15 Revenue number — generated or recovered?

Quote Capture 3 min

  • What's the single biggest difference Vini has made for your dealership?
  • What would you tell another GM or Sales Director about Vini?
💡 For revenue questions (Q15), walk in with a pre-calculated estimate based on dashboard data and average front-end gross per unit — let the customer confirm a number rather than calculate on the spot.
